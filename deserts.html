Dessert Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g sugar
  • 30g all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in melted chocolate mixture and vanilla extract.
  5. Add flour and mix gently.
  6. Pour into greased ramekins and bake for 8-10 minutes.
  7. Serve warm with ice cream.

Ingredients:

  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 50g sugar
  • 1 cup brewed espresso
  • 100g ladyfinger biscuits
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until creamy.
  2. Fold in mascarpone cheese.
  3. Dip ladyfingers in espresso and layer them in a dish.
  4. Spread mascarpone mixture over the layer.
  5. Repeat layers and dust with cocoa powder.
  6. Refrigerate for 4 hours before serving.

Ingredients:

  • 200g cream cheese
  • 100g sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g crushed graham crackers
  • 50g melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix crushed graham crackers with melted butter and press into a pan.
  3. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well.
  5. Pour over the crust and bake for 40 minutes.
  6. Let it cool and refrigerate before serving.

Ingredients:

  • 2 apples (sliced)
  • 100g sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 100g butter
  • 50ml water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix apples with sugar, cinnamon, and lemon juice.
  3. Prepare dough with flour, butter, and water.
  4. Roll out dough and place half in a pie pan.
  5. Fill with apple mixture and cover with another dough layer.
  6. Bake for 40 minutes until golden brown.

Ingredients:

  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 50g sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon gelatin
  • 2 tablespoons water

Instructions:

  1. Heat cream, sugar, and vanilla in a saucepan until warm.
  2. Dissolve gelatin in water and add to the cream mixture.
  3. Pour into molds and refrigerate for 4 hours.
  4. Serve chilled with fruit or caramel sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 50g sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (for caramelizing)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C (300°F).
  2. Heat cream and vanilla in a saucepan.
  3. Whisk egg yolks and sugar together.
  4. Gradually mix hot cream into the egg mixture.
  5. Pour into ramekins and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Cool, then sprinkle sugar on top and caramelize with a torch.

Ingredients:

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Melt chocolate and let it cool slightly.
  2. Whip cream with sugar and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  3. Gently fold in the melted chocolate.
  4. Refrigerate for 2 hours before serving.

Ingredients:

  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 100g butter
  • 50g sugar
  • 200ml custard
  • Assorted fruits (strawberries, kiwi, blueberries)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix flour, butter, and sugar to make dough.
  3. Roll out and press into a tart pan. Bake for 15 minutes.
  4. Fill with custard and top with fresh fruits.
  5. Chill before serving.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

Instructions:

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, ghee, and milk into a soft dough.
  2. Shape into small balls and fry until golden brown.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup with water, sugar, and cardamom powder.
  4. Soak fried balls in syrup for 1 hour before serving.
