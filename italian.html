Italian Cuisine

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 150g fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 250°C (480°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Tear mozzarella into small pieces and distribute over the pizza.
  5. Bake for 7-10 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil and drizzle with olive oil before serving.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions. Add minced beef and cook until browned.
  2. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Cook lasagna sheets as per package instructions.
  4. Layer sauce, lasagna sheets, and ricotta cheese in a baking dish. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30 minutes. Serve hot.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/4 tsp saffron threads
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat broth in a pot and keep warm.
  2. Sauté onions in butter until soft.
  3. Add rice and toast for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in white wine and stir until absorbed.
  5. Add broth one ladle at a time, stirring constantly.
  6. Stir in saffron, Parmesan cheese, and season to taste. Serve warm.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese (grated)
  • 50g pancetta (diced)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • Black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a bowl, mix eggs, Pecorino Romano, and black pepper.
  3. Sauté pancetta in a pan until crispy. Add garlic and cook briefly.
  4. Drain pasta and add to the pan. Remove from heat.
  5. Quickly stir in egg mixture, allowing the heat of the pasta to cook the sauce.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 2 veal shanks
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 carrot (chopped)
  • 1 celery stalk (chopped)
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season veal shanks with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear them in butter until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. Sauté onion, carrot, and celery in the same pan.
  4. Stir in tomato paste, then deglaze with white wine.
  5. Add veal shanks back, pour in broth, and cover.
  6. Simmer on low for 1.5 hours until tender. Serve hot.

Ingredients:

  • 200g tortellini (cheese or meat-filled)
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 onion (chopped)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat chicken broth in a pot.
  2. Sauté onions and garlic in a separate pan, then add to the broth.
  3. Season with nutmeg.
  4. Add tortellini and cook as per package instructions.
  5. Serve hot with grated Parmesan on top.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of Italian bread
  • 2 tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toast bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Mix tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and salt.
  3. Spoon the mixture onto toasted bread.
  4. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve.

Ingredients:

  • 500g flour
  • 300ml water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 packet dry yeast
  • Rosemary for topping

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water with sugar.
  2. Mix with flour, olive oil, and salt. Knead into a dough.
  3. Let rise for 1 hour.
  4. Flatten dough into a baking tray.
  5. Dimple surface with fingers, drizzle olive oil, and sprinkle rosemary.
  6. Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 25 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups risotto (leftover or fresh)
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella (diced)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Shape risotto into balls, placing mozzarella in the center.
  2. Coat with egg and breadcrumbs.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Drain excess oil and serve hot.

Ingredients:

  • 4 veal cutlets
  • 4 slices prosciutto
  • Fresh sage leaves
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Top veal cutlets with prosciutto and sage leaves. Secure with toothpicks.
  2. Sear veal in butter until browned on both sides.
  3. Deglaze with white wine and simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then serve hot.
