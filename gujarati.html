Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix chickpea flour, yogurt, ginger-chili paste, turmeric, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add water gradually to make a smooth batter and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Grease a steaming tray and pour the batter into it.
  4. Steam the batter for 15-20 minutes until cooked.
  5. In a separate pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds. Once they splutter, pour over the steamed dhokla.
  6. Serve hot with chutney.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Mix gram flour, buttermilk, turmeric, and salt into a smooth batter.
  2. Cook on a low flame until thick.
  3. Spread the batter on a greased surface.
  4. Once cooled, roll into thin spirals.
  5. Temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  6. Serve with chutney.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying
  • Jalebi (prepared separately)

Instructions:

  1. Mix gram flour, turmeric, salt, carom seeds, and baking soda.
  2. Add oil and enough water to knead a stiff dough.
  3. Roll thin and cut into strips.
  4. Deep fry until crisp.
  5. Serve with hot jalebis.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons gram flour (besan)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • Curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix yogurt, gram flour, and water into a smooth mixture.
  2. Boil while stirring continuously.
  3. Temper with mustard seeds, cumin seeds, green chili, and curry leaves.
  4. Add sugar and salt.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
